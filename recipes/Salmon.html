Ingredients

    Salmon fillet(s) Asparagus Rice Honey Garlic Red pepper flakes Soy Sauce Olive Oil Salt and Pepper

Steps

    Start whatever process you use for cooking rice Start heating a cast iron or carbon steel pan over medium-high heat with 1-tbsp of Olive oil coating the bottom Mix honey,Red pepper flakes, and Soy Sauce in a small bowl and set aside Pat salmon fillets dry and season with salt and pepper to taste, then place skin side up in the pre-heated skilet Cook for 6 minutes. While searing, prepare the Asparugus Flip salmon and add asparagus to the pan. Place 3 cloves minced garlic in and cook until fragrent Once garlic is fragrant pour the auce mixture in and baste for 3-4 minutes Plate the shit and enjoy!
