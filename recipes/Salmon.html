Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet(s)
  • Asparagus
  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1/4 cup Honey
  • 3 cloves minced Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup low sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Start whatever process you use for cooking rice
  2. Start heating a cast iron or carbon steel pan over medium-high heat with 1-tbsp of Olive oil coating the bottom
  3. Mix honey, Red pepper flakes, and Soy Sauce in a small bowl and set aside
  4. Pat salmon fillets dry and season with salt and pepper to taste, then place skin side up in the pre-heated skilet
  5. Cook for 6 minutes. While searing, prepare the Asparugus
  6. Flip salmon and add asparagus to the pan. Place 3 cloves minced garlic in and cook until fragrent
  7. Once garlic is fragrant pour the sauce mixture in and baste for 3-4 minutes
  8. Plate and enjoy!
